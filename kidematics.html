They focus on providing a structured before/afterschool experience with physical activity and social skills lessons. Each day the children rotate through different stations and are taught weekly lessons using our curriculum guide. Our before/afterschool programs provide a safe and structured environment for the children and encourage children to meet and exceed daily physical activity levels while also building healthy relationships and life skills. *We DO NOT operate on snow days or when schools are closed.
